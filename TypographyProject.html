From typography project, I learnt there are 8 types of typographic systems.

  • Axial

Axial typographic system mean elements are arranged on either side of line or axis. From the above, it can seen that the axis formed by the title and 2 main paragraph seperated by the axis.

  • Radial

Radial typographic system mean all elements extend from a point, then form rays.

  • Dilatational

Dilatational typographic system is the easiest one, just think about circle. From my design, I made 2 pairs of eyes first, then put text around them.

  • Random

Random typographic system just like what it's name, all elements arranged randomly in my design.

  • Grid

Grid typographic system mean that devide the page into sections and help organize content. From my work, it is devided into 10 section, formed by 9*9 square and bottom section.

  • Modular

Modular typographic system use repeating structures to break up the content. For my modular design, I got inspiration from stair, then organized the stroke and put texts inside.

  • Transitional

Transitional typographic system is to show the movement of texts, in my work, I tried to organized the texts looks like sea, and adding bracket to formed birds also by adding shapes and line to made a sun.

  • Bilateral

Bilateral typographic system is the simpplest one, anothere name for it is centered, just like what center mean, only need to arrange all the things into center.
